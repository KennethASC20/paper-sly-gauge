What Is This Site?

Die2Gether is a dating website dedicated to those who are elderly and still looking for a significant other to be a companion to. We all know that at a young age, love can be a hard thing to obtain but not when you're in youre mid 50's. This site is still in development and being worked on around the clock to bring satisfying features that will hopefully bring a more settling atmosphere.

Where Can You Access This Site?

Currently, this site can only be access through the web. It's very compatible with Chrome and Internet Explorer, but be cautious about other third party browsers such as FireFox, Safari, Opera, etc. Hopefully in the future, Die2Gether will become an app software to the public and be used world wide.

I made the foundations of this site using an in browser coding application known as Glitch.
